Our Purpose

Creating Community

Our thought in creating this website was to create a stronger sense of community. We purposefully chose to create this through digital means as many can argue that the internet has ruined our sense of community. Computers, along with the rise of technology, have impacted society and changed the way we interact with each other forever and, in many ways, has made us more anti-social. We were trying to explore ways of making a space where people of a certain community could interact with each other and help each other thrive. More specifically, we want to explore ways that the internet can help solve immediate problems on a local scale, like Stevens or any University.

Promoting Inclusivity

We created a website in which users can create "complaints" and post their concerns publicly to their community's dashboard without fear of penalty. In our personal experience, if there is a problem around campus, it's difficult as a single person to amplify one's voice, especially if the problem affects a select few or an underrepresented group. More often than not, when an issue it brought up, it typically takes a few weeks to months for it to be addressed as it's only 'one' person's complaint. However, we are hoping that, in creating this anonymous site, every individual will feel more comfortable speaking their mind in a post, especially with "bigger picture" problems that need more immediate effect (cough cough the Burchard building's bathroom situation). So, by having an online outlet where many people can easily discuss and show their support through likes and messages, we are hoping that it opens the door to a more inclusive campus and safe space for everyone.

Overall Goal

This would ideally make matters that currently have trouble getting any traction more pressing to the administrators that need to oversee that change as it is more publicized. We will look into flaws in other sites that could have that potential, and we will also look into the stigmas of how people react to change and what goes into making an idea come to fruition.
